Segway'n USA

My first day of the trip!    I can't believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go.    Because I'm on a Segway I won't be able to bring a whole lot with me:   cell phone,   iPod, digital camera and a protein bar.    Just the essentials.    As Lao Tzu would have said,   A journey of a thousand miles begines with one step Segway.
